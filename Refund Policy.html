Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At Khel Yogdaan, we strive to ensure fair play and transparent transactions. Please read the following policies carefully to understand how refunds and cancellations work on our platform.

üéÆ Match Cancellations (By Admin)

If a match is cancelled by the Khel Yogdaan team due to server issues, lack of participants, or technical glitches:

  • You will receive a 100% Refund of your entry fee (Coins).
  • The refund will be credited to your in-app wallet instantly or within 24 hours.
  • No action is required from your side.

üë§ Cancellations by User

If you have joined a match but wish to leave:

  • You can unregister from a match up to 15 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • In this case, the entry fee will be fully refunded to your wallet.
  • Note: If you leave within 15 minutes of the match start time, NO REFUND will be provided as the slot was reserved for you.

üö´ Disqualification & Bans

We have a zero-tolerance policy for cheating. No refunds will be issued in the following cases:

  • If you are found using Hacks, Scripts, or Emulators.
  • If you are caught Teaming up with opponents in Solo/Duo matches.
  • If you fail to join the room on time (Room ID/Pass is provided 10 mins prior).
  • If your internet connection fails during the game.

üí≥ Deposit/Transaction Failures

If you paid money via UPI/Gateway but coins were not added to your wallet:

  • Please wait for 30 minutes as bank servers might be slow.
  • If the issue persists, the money is usually auto-refunded by your bank within 3-5 business days.
  • You can contact our support with your Transaction/UTR ID for manual verification.

üè¶ Withdrawals

Coins won in tournaments can be withdrawn to UPI (PhonePe, GPay) or Redeem Codes.

  • Requests are processed within 24 to 48 hours.
  • If you provide incorrect UPI details, Khel Yogdaan is not responsible for the loss of funds.
  • Once processed, withdrawals cannot be reversed.
